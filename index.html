Students inspiring companies since 2011.

Open Summer of Code's intensive program takes 20 to 30 students under its wing in the Belgian cities of Kortrijk, Antwerp and Ghent during the summer of 2014. The goal: to provide them the training, network and support necessary to transform 6 open innovation projects into powerful real-world services in three weeks.
